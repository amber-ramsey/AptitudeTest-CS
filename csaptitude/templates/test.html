Computer Programming Aptitude Test

Test Instructions

The test has 31 questions and you will have 30 minutes to do them. At the end of the test (when 30 minutes have elapsed), you will be given a score. Please use SCRAP PAPER and a CALCULATOR for working out answers. Please note that this is quite a demanding test.

The test will start with three easy example questions, which will not be marked or timed. Click on the "First Example" button to begin. Click on your answer to answer it. You can change your answer by clicking on another answer. You can also go back to the previous question by clicking on the previous question button. If you are certain you have finished the test, you can click on "Finish Test and Get Your Score".
